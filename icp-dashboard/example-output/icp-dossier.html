What they want vs. what they fear

Desires
  • Margin on every retainer, with no new monthly cost
  • Tools they can rebrand and resell to clients
  • To look like a full team while staying solo
  • Reliability over features: "will I still use it in 6 months?"
  • One-time pricing they own, not seats they rent
Pains
  • Overhead and the pressure to hire (the margin killer)
  • Recurring SaaS bills that quietly eat profit
  • Looking small next to bigger competitors
  • Licensing friction across multiple client accounts
  • Time lost to manual, repetitive work
